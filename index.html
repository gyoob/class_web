What is STUDIO GHIBLI?

 Studio Ghibli Co., Ltd. is a Japanese company whose main business is planning and producing animation-oriented video works. Its main business is the production of feature-length animated films, and since the mid-1990s, it has been involved in the production of short works and the planning of live-action works.

 The painting style is so different from the mainstream animation style that anyone who has watched Ghibli's movie two to three times can feel "It's Ghibli's work" even if they pass by, but the line is more lyrical than neat. These characteristics can be seen well by looking at the sky in the red pig and the natural scenery in Mononoke Hime. In Japan, this style of painting seems to be called "eojeonghwa," which is one of the main reasons why works that have been released more than 20 years with Laputa or Totoro are still long running because they tend to ride less time.
