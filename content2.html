Ghibli's background music
that everyone loves

 Ghibli's film is famous for its soundtrack, which is a perfect match for the work, along with its cinematic beauty, direction, and story. "My Neighbor Totoro," "The Merry-Go-Round of Life," and "Always and Again" are songs that everyone knows even if they don't know the names. Listening to the Ghibli OST reminds me of the scene of the movie, and even "I" at the time of watching the movie comes along. I felt a sense of rest, the ecstasy I felt at the time, the warmth of the past, and the familiar scenery. Music welcomes the past.
